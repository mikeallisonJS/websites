The funkiest analog saw-square hybrid, multi-sampled synth with enough bark to knock out a stringed bass.


The racks also give you overdrive, delay, huge gated reverbs, OTT compression,  a Low Pass and a High Pass Filter to achieve an insanely wide variety of sounds.

Each key from C0-C3 is sampled individually to provide you with the true analog oscillator fluctuations. 

Includes 2 versions for Ableton Live Suite & Standard
