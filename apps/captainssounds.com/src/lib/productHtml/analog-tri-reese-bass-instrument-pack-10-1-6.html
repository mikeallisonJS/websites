This dual-oscillator, multi-sampled, analog FM Reese bass will shake you like a THX intro. 
The racks also give you overdrive, a 2nd notch LFO (Suite only), huge reverb, noise, OTT compression, Multiband EQ for clean subs, and a Pre-fx High Pass to achieve an insanely wide variety of sounds.

Each key from C0-C2 is sampled individually to provide you with the true analog oscillator fluctuations. 

The 4 example audio files have no additional processing, and are straight out of the rack!

Includes 2 versions for Ableton Live Suite & Standard
