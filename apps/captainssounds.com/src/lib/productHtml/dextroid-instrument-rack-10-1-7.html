This rack uses 3 free vsts to create wild, mangled screaming FM basses & leads. Really, it's damn near infinite what you could do with it. I could play with this for days (and I have).

Just grab the 3 free vsts and the rack should pop up in the latest Ableton 10.1. If you aren't using ableton, you can still easily recreate this strip using your favorite saturator and the free vst version of OTT.

Required VST links:

https://asb2m10.github.io/dexed/

https://polyversemusic.com/products/wider/

https://www.plugin-alliance.com/en/products/bx_subfilter.html

Alternate strip vst links (if you're not in Ableton):

https://www.softube.com/saturationknob

https://xferrecords.com/freeware

Hope you guys get some great use out of this!
