Analog multi-sampled Minibrute 2S sub patches for Ableton Live 10.1.6+. 

Includes:
MB2S Sub Simpler Instrument
MB2S Sub Sampler Instrument
Rattlesnake MB2S Sub Rack

 

Each key in the 2 Octave (C0-C2) Sub range is individually sampled.

The sub was produced on an Arturia Minibrute 2S using 2 oscillators creating a very powerful, wobbly sine wave with slight harmonics to make it a perfect base for any sub bass patch.

The sounds of this instrument and it's samples are 100% Royalty Free for use in commercial Music or Video. They are not to be re-sold or re-sampled as part of another sample pack or instrument without explicit licensing.
