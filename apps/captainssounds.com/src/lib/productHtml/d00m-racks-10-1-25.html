This is it. The ultimate sound mangling, shredding, sound design racks.
Loosely based off of Mick Gordon's work in designing the Doom soundtrack (see below), each of these racks create 4 channels of audio destruction. Each of those four are capable of additionally being mixed or sidechained as inputs to any other channel, creating endless processing and feedback capabilities.
