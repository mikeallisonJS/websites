Dual-oscillator, multi-sampled, analog synth just dripping with smooth retro sounds

The racks also give you overdrive, auto-pan, chorus, huge reverb, OTT compression, and a Low Pass Filter to achieve an insanely wide variety of sounds.

Each key from C0-C9 is sampled individually to provide you with the true analog oscillator fluctuations. 

Includes 2 versions for Ableton Live Suite & Standard
